Our story

Created in 1812, VSL Boulangerie is the oldest bakery in Finland.

We use our expertise to combine French craftsmanship with local Nordic ingredients. We value slow fermentation, seasonal flavors and careful technique.

The Bakery

Everything is baked on-site, daily. We focus on sustainability: local grains, minimal waste, and recyclable packaging.

Our Process

Long fermentation, hand-shaped loaves, classic pastry lamination — we respect tradition and experiment with quality ingredients.
